Healthy Oates

Oats are an incredibly nutritious food packed with important vitamins, minerals and antioxidants. In addition, they're high in fiber and protein compared to other grains.

INGREDIENT for people

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 medium banana,mashed
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoon cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine all ingredients into a small saucepan and turn heat to meduim/high
  2. Bring to a boil. Then, turn heat downto low/medium and stir for around 3-5 minutes as theoatmeal coocks and thickens.
  3. Once oatmeal is at desired consistency, remove from heat and serve immidantely
